# HireVue

**Category:** Video Interview Platform  
**Format:** Recorded video responses with AI analysis  
**Assessment:** 🔴 High Concern

---

## What They Do

HireVue records your video responses to behavioral interview questions and uses AI to analyze:
- Facial expressions and micro-expressions
- Tone of voice and speech patterns
- Word choice and content
- Body language and movement

The AI scores you before a human may (or may not) review your application.

---

## Why This Is Particularly Problematic

**Direct Discrimination Against Neurodivergent Traits:**

**Facial Expression Analysis:**
- Autistic flat affect scored as "not engaged" or "not enthusiastic"
- Difficulty with eye contact penalized
- Neutral expressions read as "negative"
- Micro-expressions may not match internal state

**Tone and Speech Analysis:**
- Monotone speech (common in autism) scored lower
- Atypical prosody flagged as "lacking confidence"
- Speech rate differences penalized
- Vocal stimming may be analyzed negatively

**Body Language Scoring:**
- Stimming flagged as "nervous" or "unprofessional"
- ADHD fidgeting scored as "distracted" or "anxious"
- Looking away (avoiding eye contact) = "not interested"
- Rigid posture or repetitive movements flagged

**Word Choice Analysis:**
- Literal, direct answers may not match "expected" patterns
- Detailed explanations seen as "over-explaining"
- Different narrative structures penalized
- AI trained on neurotypical communication

---

## What to Expect

**Format:**
- Record yourself answering pre-set questions
- Usually 30 seconds to prepare
- 1-3 minutes to answer each question
- 5-10 questions total
- Cannot pause or take breaks

**Questions:**
- Behavioral ("Tell me about a time...")
- Situational ("What would you do if...")
- Motivation/culture fit

**Evaluation:**
- AI scores your video before human review
- You may be eliminated before anyone watches your full video
- Scoring criteria often opaque

---

## Legal and Ethical Concerns

**Lawsuits and Bans:**
- Multiple discrimination lawsuits filed
- Illinois banned facial analysis features
- Maryland restricted AI video interview analysis
- ACLU and other organizations have raised concerns

**Lack of Scientific Validity:**
- No proof facial expressions predict job performance
- Pseudo-scientific "emotion detection"
- Training data likely biased
- Reinforces existing discrimination

**Privacy Issues:**
- Recording and analyzing biometric data
- Unclear data retention and usage
- May be used for purposes beyond initial interview

## Accommodation Strategies

**STRONGLY REQUEST ALTERNATIVE:**

Send to HR before assessment:

"I have [autism/ADHD] and HireVue's AI analysis discriminates against neurodivergent communication patterns. As a reasonable accommodation under the ADA, I request:

1. Live phone interview instead of video recording
2. Human-only evaluation (no AI analysis)
3. Written responses to questions
4. Alternative demonstration of qualifications

I'm happy to demonstrate my qualifications through methods that don't rely on neurotypical facial expressions and speech patterns."

**If they refuse:**
- Document the refusal
- Consider filing ADA complaint if applicable
- Seriously reconsider this company

**If you must proceed:**
- Understand you're at systematic disadvantage
- Focus on content of answers
- Practice with video (though it won't fix AI bias)
- Consider this a preview of company culture

---

## Alternative Approaches to Propose

"Instead of HireVue, could we:
- Have a traditional phone conversation?
- Do a live video call where we can interact?
- I provide written responses you can review?
- Schedule an in-person interview?"

**Frame as:**
"I want to demonstrate my actual qualifications, which facial analysis software cannot accurately assess for neurodivergent candidates."

---

## Serious Red Flags

**If company:**
- Refuses any accommodation
- Insists HireVue is "objective" or "unbiased"
- Won't explain what the AI measures
- Uses HireVue as sole screening method
- Dismisses concerns about discrimination

**This tells you:**
- They prioritize automation over finding good candidates
- They don't understand neurodiversity
- They likely won't accommodate you as an employee
- Run, don't walk, away from this opportunity

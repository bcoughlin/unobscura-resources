# Video Interview Platforms

Platforms that use video recording or live video interviews, sometimes with AI analysis of facial expressions, tone, and body language.

## Platforms in This Category

### 🔴 High Concern
- **[HireVue](hirevue.md)** - AI facial/emotion analysis

## Common Challenges

**Video Format:**
- Sensory overwhelm from seeing yourself on screen
- Difficulty maintaining "appropriate" eye contact with camera
- Awkwardness of talking to a camera vs. person
- No conversational feedback

**AI Analysis (HireVue):**
- Facial expression analysis discriminates against autistic flat affect
- Tone/prosody analysis penalizes monotone speech
- Body language scoring penalizes stimming, fidgeting
- Algorithms trained on neurotypical patterns

**Time Pressure:**
- Limited time to prepare (often 30 seconds)
- Limited time to respond (1-3 minutes)
- No accommodation for processing speed differences
- Can't take breaks to regulate

## General Accommodation Strategies

**Request:**
- Phone call instead of video
- Live conversation instead of one-way recording
- Human-only evaluation (no AI analysis)
- Extended time for preparation and responses

**Alternative Proposals:**
- Written responses to questions
- Portfolio review with Q&A
- Asynchronous video where you can re-record
- Traditional phone screen

## Legal Considerations

**Some jurisdictions have banned AI facial analysis in hiring:**
- Illinois banned HireVue's facial analysis
- Maryland restricted AI video interview analysis
- More regulations likely coming

**ADA Accommodations:**
- Requesting phone vs. video is reasonable
- Requesting no AI analysis is reasonable
- Alternative demonstration of qualifications

## Red Flags

**Reconsider if company:**
- Insists on AI video analysis despite concerns
- Won't provide alternative to video format
- Can't explain what AI is measuring
- Claims video analysis is "scientifically validated"

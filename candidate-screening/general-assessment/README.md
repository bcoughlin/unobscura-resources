# General Assessment Platforms

Platforms that assess cognitive abilities, personality traits, or behavioral characteristics through games, questionnaires, or other non-job-specific tests.

## Platforms in This Category

### ⚠️ Significant Challenges
- **[Pymetrics](pymetrics.md)** - Neuroscience-based cognitive games

## Common Challenges

**Trait Matching:**
- Assumes one "right" cognitive profile per role
- Neurodivergent processing styles may not fit templates
- Cognitive diversity seen as mismatch, not strength
- Fixed view of abilities vs. accommodation potential

**Timed Games:**
- Processing speed differences penalized
- Fast ≠ good, slow ≠ bad
- No accommodation for different processing speeds
- Thoughtful approaches scored lower

**"Culture Fit" Proxies:**
- Personality assessments may code for neurotype
- Social cognition games favor neurotypical patterns
- Emotion recognition tests disadvantage some autistic people
- "Fit" often means conformity

**Unclear Job Relevance:**
- Games may not predict actual job performance
- Cognitive traits measured ≠ skills needed
- Research on validity often questionable
- Used as cheap filter, not valid assessment

## General Accommodation Strategies

**Request:**
- Untimed versions of assessments
- Explanation of what's being measured and why
- Evidence linking assessment to job performance
- Alternative demonstration of relevant skills

**Question:**
- "How were these cognitive profiles determined?"
- "What's the evidence these predict job success?"
- "How do you account for neurodiversity?"
- "Are accommodations available?"

**Alternative Proposals:**
- Job simulation or work sample
- Portfolio review
- Trial project
- Skills-based assessment

## Red Flags

**Reconsider if company:**
- Can't explain what's being measured
- Claims assessments are "scientifically proven" without evidence
- Uses results as primary filter
- Won't accommodate different processing styles
- Treats cognitive diversity as deficiency

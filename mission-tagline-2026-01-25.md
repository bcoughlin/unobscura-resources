## Unobscura

**Date:** January 25, 2026  
**Version:** 2026-01-25

---

## Tagline

The world can appear upside down when seen through eyes of someone different than our own. Our mission is to reveal the wonder of the human mind by bringing awareness to our own neurotype and that of others. We seek to unobscure the many variations of thinking and sensing to make humanity more harmonious and equitable.

---

## Usage

This tagline serves as:
- Elevator pitch / quick summary
- Introduction to the organization's purpose
- Explanation of the "Unobscura" name (camera obscura metaphor)

**Metaphor:** Like a camera obscura creates an inverted image, different neurotypes experience the world through fundamentally different orientations—what's "right side up" for one is naturally "upside down" for another, and neither is the "correct" orientation.

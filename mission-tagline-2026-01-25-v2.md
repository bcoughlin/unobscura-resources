## Unobscura

**Date:** January 25, 2026  
**Version:** 2.0 (2026-01-25-v2)

---

## Tagline

We believe that different doesn't mean broken. The gaps in our understanding of each other's cognitive styles create frustration, confusion, and mistrust—limiting our collective potential. Our mission is to bridge these gaps by bringing awareness to our neurotypes. We seek to unobscure the many variations of thinking and sensing to make humanity more harmonious and equitable.

---

## Usage

This tagline serves as:
- Elevator pitch / quick summary
- Introduction to the organization's purpose
- Values statement ("different doesn't mean broken")

**Key Message:** Neurotype blindness costs ALL of humanity—not just neurodivergent people—by creating unnecessary friction and preventing us from reaching our collective potential through mutual understanding.
